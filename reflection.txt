Reflection

While designing this landing page, I used animations to guide the user's attention rather than decorating every element. The floating animation in the hero section makes the page feel more dynamic, while the scroll reveal effect highlights the features only when they come into view. I kept the animations short and smooth so they enhance the user experience without becoming distracting. This approach helped create a professional-looking interface that remains easy to navigate.
